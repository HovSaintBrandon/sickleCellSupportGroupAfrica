About Sickle Cell Disease

Knowledge is the First Step to Change

Sickle cell disease is the most common inherited blood disorder in Africa. By spreading awareness and providing support, we can improve outcomes for millions.

Understanding SCD

Sickle cell disease affects the red blood cells, causing them to become rigid and sickle-shaped, leading to pain and complications.

Genetic Awareness

SCD is inherited when both parents carry the sickle cell trait. Genetic counseling can help families make informed decisions.

Community Support

We connect patients, families, and healthcare providers to build a strong support network across African communities.

Prevention & Care

Early screening, proper treatment, and preventive care can significantly improve quality of life for those with SCD.
